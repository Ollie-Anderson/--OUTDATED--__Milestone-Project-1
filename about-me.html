Me As A Developer

I have a passion for Designing games that stems from a love of the media itself. I like to look deep into the psychology and theory behind what makes a good game and what makes experiences meaningful. I believe that the desire to more deeply understand the abstract side of the game design is what drives my decision making as a developer, and a core that underpins all of my work.

I initially pursued programming through software development and computer science, giving me a good understanding of the purer applications of programming. This has left me with experience developing for many applications resultin in clean and efficient code, well thought out systems, and developer-friendly assets.

The theoretical basis for my skills means I produce consistent work regardless of the tools/engine, and i am a fast and adaptable worker helping me to quickly acclimatise to any team. I am used to uswing kanban and agile frameworks for my development workflow, but can produce work inline with any team's style/philosophy.

My Love For Games

When it comes to the specifics, my passion lies in player driven experinces. I love creating system based games that allow player driven, emergent narrative. I think games like this allow players to create deeper personal connections to what they're experiencing, and create lasting memories.

That being said, I am passionate about games in every form, however big or small. I love trying new things, experimenting with the media, and learning as much as I can along the way. If you think I could benefit your team or project, please dont hesitate to get in touch. I would love to be a part of what you're workign on.
